My Hometown!

Hi beautiful People! This is my lovely hometown. My hometown is in Pandak Gede, Tabanan, the capital town of the regency of Bali. I love visiting my hometown because I can really feel the Bali vibes in there. Like most regional capitals in Bali, Tabanan is a large, well-organised place. One thing i love about my hometown is the magical and natural atmoshphere that it's brought! Even The majority of the area consists of rice fields and plantations, and as a result, is the major center of rice production on the island.

Hope to see you there!
